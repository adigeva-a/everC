Root Cause

The bottleneck is not data access.
It's how investigations are structured.

Each finding arrives as an isolated data point
No guidance on what internal data matters
Constant context switching across violation types
Open-ended, high-friction investigation

G2RS cannot eliminate the investigation — but it can eliminate the inefficiencies around it.

Risk Management

Risks & Mitigations

We've identified four key risks and how we address each.

High
Adoption friction
Analysts resist changing established workflows. New tools add cognitive overhead before reducing it.
→ Co-design with analysts from Phase 0. Weekly feedback loops. Gradual feature rollout — not a big bang.
Medium
AI summary quality
Inaccurate or generic summaries erode trust. Analysts may ignore AI guidance entirely.
→ Human-in-the-loop validation. Summaries as starting points, not conclusions. Quality improves via learning loop.
Medium
Risk tier miscategorization
Incorrect tiering could suppress alerts for high-risk merchants — regulatory and trust risk.
→ Tiers introduced in Phase 3, after Co-Pilot data validates categorization. Conservative defaults. Override controls.
Low
Integration complexity
API customers may resist workflow changes. UI changes may not fit existing toolchains.
→ Co-Pilot works as a layer on top of existing delivery (API and UI). Additive, not disruptive.
